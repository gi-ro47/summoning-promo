Summoning by GI-RO47

Seven Techno tracks spanning groovy rhythms to darker realms. Each track is crafted with raw hardware sounds, and grooves built meticulously from the ground up.

The first three tracks explore familiar Techno soundscapes, subtly inspired by nature, while the remaining tracks venture into more experimental grooves, yet still remain true to the Techno aesthetic.

This album will be released on Diffuse Reality on the 15th of December.
